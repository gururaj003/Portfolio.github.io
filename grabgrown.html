Project description

"A Web Application to provide a bridge of communication between the farmers and customers accross the Country."
E-Trading of Agricultural Products from Farmer to Customer portal.
It is a multi user system.
It consists of three section:
Farmers to sell their product: Farmers can update and delete their products regularly and sell their product on Large scale and Small scale.
Customers to buy products: Customers like market vendors, Large scale buyers, and small scale buyers are categorized. The customer are able to buy the product from the farmers nearby Location.
Delivery: The third party delivery system to deliver the products from farmer to customer for commodity wise buyers.
Features:
• Farmers can get updated on Market Prices on Daily Basis.
• Customers can buy the products by Commodity-wise and Market-wise.
• The third party delivery system for Large scale orders.
• 24/7 Online Support for farmers
• The web portal is available on Multiple Languages.

Tech Stack

Frontend: HTML, CSS, Javascrpit, Ajax
Backend:PHP
